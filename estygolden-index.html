About EstyGolden

Where Beauty Meets Artistry

EstyGolden Salon was born from a passion to celebrate every woman's natural beauty. We specialize in all forms of female hair braiding, styling, professional makeup artistry, and luxury nail care all under one elegant roof.

Our team of certified beauty professionals brings years of experience and genuine love for the craft. We believe every woman deserves to leave our salon feeling like the royalty she truly is.
